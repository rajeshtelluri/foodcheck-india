Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

FoodCheck India ("we", "the app") is a free tool that helps you check packaged food products using barcode data from Open Food Facts. This page explains what information we collect, why, and how it's handled.

What we collect

If you sign in with Google: we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from your Google account, via Firebase Authentication (a Google service). This is used only to personalize your experience (showing your name/photo in the app) and is not shared with any third party or used for advertising.

If you use the app as a guest: we don't collect any personal identity information at all.

Scan history, favorites, and preferences (like your language choice or allergy/diet settings) are stored only in your browser's local storage, directly on your device. We do not currently store this data on our own servers, and we cannot see it.

Third-party services we use

  • Open Food Facts — an open, community-run food database. When you scan or search for a product, your device sends the barcode or search term directly to their public API to retrieve product information. See their own privacy practices at openfoodfacts.org/privacy.
  • Google Sign-In / Firebase Authentication — used only if you choose to sign in.
  • Vercel — our hosting provider, which may log standard technical information (like IP address) as part of serving the website, as most web hosts do.

What we don't do

We don't sell your data. We don't show ads. We don't track you across other websites or apps. We don't share your Google account information with anyone.

Your choices

You can use the app fully as a guest without signing in. You can clear your scan history and favorites at any time from the Profile screen. You can sign out at any time, which stops your name/photo from being shown in the app.

Children's privacy

FoodCheck India is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Since this is an early-stage, free project, we may add features (like optional cloud sync of your history) in the future — if we do, we'll update this policy to describe exactly what that involves before it applies to you.

Contact us

Questions about your data or this policy? Email us at foodcheckindia.support@gmail.com.
